Tattarimäki design

Surface Patterns Inspired by Nordic Nature

Tattarimäki Design creates surface patterns inspired by Nordic Nature, Jazz Music and sometimes my two little dogs and other animals.

I use different painting techniques, mostly water colours. Tattarimäki is the name of our little country cottage in southern Finland.

Please do not hesitate to contact if you desire some other colour, scale, product etc.
